Steak!

Steak is one of the best meals you can eat! I enjoy eating steak with a refreshing beverage on a beautiful summer night.

One of the many benefits to cooking a steak is how simple it can be.

All you need is a simple grill, a couple ingredients and a good recipe.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Ribeye
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Let the steak come to room temperature - take it out of the refrigerator at least 30 minutes before you want to cook it - preheat the grill on high for at least ten minutes - screaming hot!!
  2. Season as desired, place on the grill over high heat and close the lid. Wait exactly one minute.
  3. Turn the meat 90 degrees with tongs, never use a fork! Close the lid and wait one minute more. This makes those professional-looking hash marks.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Move to an indirect heat source - turn off the burner under the steaks but leave the others on high - you want the grill temp to be about 400 degrees and cook 4 minutes on each side for medium-rare. If you like your steak more than medium-rare (and we certainly don't recommend this), continue cooking on indirect heat and use an instant-read thermometer to achieve the desired temperature.
  6. Let the steak rest under foil for 5-10 minutes. We recommend slicing against the grain before serving.

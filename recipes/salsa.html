Salsa!

Salsa is a great addition to almost any meal. It works as a appetizer, or a side dish.

Salsa is a traditional mexican food. There is a wide verity of salsas that can be made with many different unique Ingredients. Today I am going to teach you how to make Homemade Red Salsa.

Fresh Salsa Ingredients

  • Fresh Roma tomatoes
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Jalapeno
  • Red onion
  • Green onion
  • Garlic
  • Lime
  • Chili powder and cumin
  • Sugar
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Roughly chop the vegetables (and fruit if you want to get technical, those tomatoes).
  2. Add vegetables to a food processor along with spices and lime juice.
